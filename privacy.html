Privacy Policy

NiO Pictures is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Effective date: March 1, 2026  ·  NiO Pictures, Katy, Texas

1. Who We Are

NiO Pictures is a photography business operated by Palanivel, based in Katy, Texas. We provide event and family photography services to clients in Katy, Houston, Sugar Land, Cypress, Richmond, and surrounding areas.

Contact: niopictureskaty@gmail.com  ·  281-409-3585  ·  Katy, TX

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit a contact or booking inquiry on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • The message or session details you provide

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and discuss photography services. It is not stored on our servers — it is sent directly to our email inbox via Formspree (our form processing service).

Information collected automatically

When you visit niopictures.com, Google Analytics 4 (GA4) automatically collects:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • How you arrived at the site (search, direct, social media)
  • Your general geographic location (city/country level — not your precise address)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate session duration and interactions

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify you personally from this data. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish between visits.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to photography inquiries and booking requests
  • To understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • To understand which services and content are most useful to our clients

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4, provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics collects data through cookies placed on your device. This data is processed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on, or by using your browser's privacy settings to block cookies.

We have configured Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled. We do not share Google Analytics data with third parties.

5. Cookies

Our website uses the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat) — used to distinguish visitors and measure site usage. These expire after 2 years (_ga) or 24 hours (_gid).
  • Session preference — a small sessionStorage entry that remembers whether you have seen the chat widget, so it doesn't re-appear on every page. This is not a cookie and is not sent to any server.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics (Alphabet Inc.) — website analytics
  • Formspree — contact and booking form processing
  • Pixieset — online gallery and print ordering platform
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's CDN

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

7. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not share your contact details with other businesses or advertisers.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process.

8. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox as long as is necessary to manage our correspondence. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us at niopictureskaty@gmail.com.

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted by Google.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent at any time where consent is the basis for processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at niopictureskaty@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our forms, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
