Common Questions

Event Photography FAQs

Do you photograph Indian and South Asian events in Katy TX? +

Yes. NiO Pictures specializes in Indian and South Asian cultural events — garba nights, sangeet, receptions, birthday milestones, and community celebrations throughout Katy, Houston, and Sugar Land, TX.

How far in advance should I book event photography? +

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks ahead for private events, and 2–3 months for larger corporate events or cultural celebrations to secure your date.

Do you cover events in Houston and Sugar Land, not just Katy? +

Absolutely. NiO Pictures is based in Katy TX but actively serves Houston, Sugar Land, and Richmond for all event types — corporate, private, and cultural.

What is included in event photography coverage? +

Every package includes full or half-day coverage, professionally edited images in our cinematic style, and delivery via a private online gallery within 2–3 weeks.

Are you available on weekends and weeknights? +

Yes. NiO Pictures offers photography sessions on weekends and weeknight evenings throughout Katy, Houston, Sugar Land, and Richmond TX.
